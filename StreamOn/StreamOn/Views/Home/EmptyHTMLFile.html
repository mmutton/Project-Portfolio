Wireless Speakers

  • Sonos $$ Sonos did to the home stereo what music streaming did to radio: killed it! Sonos is easy to set up and use, looks and sounds good, and works in multiple rooms. Some models come ready with the Google Assistant, "Alexa")
  • Devialet Phantom $$$$ This speaker is spendy, but the Devialet Phantom is packing 3000W of power and produces "mind-bending sound quality". Kinda looks like an egg that's gonna hatch R2D2 out of it.
  • Bose SoundTouch $$ Bose entered into the game with several models to choose from. All are easy to set up and control and offer the sound quality you'd expect from Bose.
  • Samsung Radiant360 $$-$$$ Have a need for omni-directional sound? The wait is over. Available in three models with different size and sound capacities, the Radiant 360 is pretty nifty if your speaker is in the middle of a room.
  • BlueSound Pulse Mini $$$ If high-definition audio is a must, the BlueSound Pulse Mini might just be your gadget. With higher sample rates and bit depths transmitted wirelessly, you're gonna want to match this speaker up with Tidal for the ultimate Hi-Def sound.
